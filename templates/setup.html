Relay Configuration

Configure the GPIO pins connected to your relay board. Uses BCM (Broadcom) pin numbering.

BCM pin → Relay 1 → Modem
BCM pin → Relay 2 → Router
BCM pin for manual reset pushbutton
Check your relay board datasheet. Most common boards are active HIGH.

Email Alerts

NetWatch can email you when the network goes down or resets. Uses Gmail with an App Password.

You'll need a Gmail App Password — not your regular Gmail password. Go to myaccount.google.com → Security → App Passwords to create one.
16-character app password from Google Account settings
Where alert emails are delivered (can be any email address)
